FAQ

Simple answers for first-time visitors.

Can I book through Clinic Finder?

No. The homepage is designed around discovery and price comparison, then sending people directly to the clinic once they choose an option.

Who is this for?

People with no insurance, high-deductible plans, freelance coverage gaps, or anyone trying to compare self-pay care before reaching out.

What kinds of care can be listed?

Primary care, specialist consultations, labs, imaging, outpatient procedures, wellness visits, and other routine self-pay healthcare categories.
